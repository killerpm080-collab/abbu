FAQ

Built-in answers for the most important pre-sale questions your customers will ask.

Can customers pay from any platform?

They can choose their preferred source in the order form, including cards, banks, PayPal, Wise, crypto, or mobile wallets. The website then records the order so you can confirm and reconcile it through your Payoneer process.

Does this directly connect to live Payoneer checkout?

No. Payoneer is usually better treated as the account you receive and reconcile funds through. This site gives you a working order and admin system now, and you can later connect a specialized gateway or backend if needed.
